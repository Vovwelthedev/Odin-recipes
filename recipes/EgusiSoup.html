Egusi Soup Recipes

Egusi soup is a popular West African dish made with ground melon seeds, vegetables, and meat or fish. It is known for its rich and hearty flavor, and is often served with fufu or rice.

Ingredients

  • Melon
  • Vegetables
  • Palm Oil
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Blend Melon and Tomatoes
  2. Heat Oil
  3. Fry everything
  4. Add Water
  5. Boil
