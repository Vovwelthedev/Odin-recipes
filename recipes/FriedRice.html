Fried Rice Recipe

This is a simple and delicious fried rice recipe that you can make at home. It's perfect for using up leftover rice and can be customized with your favorite vegetables and proteins.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Beat the eggs and pour them into the skillet. Scramble until fully cooked.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce. Stir well to combine all ingredients.
