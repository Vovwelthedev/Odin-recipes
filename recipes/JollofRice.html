Jollof Rice Recipe

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a variety of spices. It's a flavorful and vibrant dish that is often served at parties and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cups chicken broth

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, paprika, thyme, and curry powder. Cook for about 5 minutes until the tomatoes break down and the spices are fragrant.
  4. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir to coat it with the tomato mixture.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
